I am a CSE PhD student at UC San Diego, advised by Professor Ravi Ramamoorthi. In the past, I have had the fortune to work at the Max Planck Institute and the University of Oxford. Most recently, I spent 1.5 years at NVIDIA AI working with Koki Nagano.

I'm lucky to be supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship and Jacobs School of Engineering Fellowship.

I'm currently at Google DeepMind in San Francisco as a Student Researcher with Pratul Srinivasan and Ben Poole.
