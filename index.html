GRF: Learning a General Radiance Field for 3D Scene Representation and Rendering
Alex Trevithick, Bo Yang
arXiv, 2020
project page / arXiv / video

We learn per-pixel pose-aware features representing all the points along the light ray from the viewpoint through the image plane at a pixel. Using attention, we aggregate features from multiple views for each point in 3D space and apply volumetric rendering. Our approach can generalize to high-resolution novel scenes in a single forward pass and achieves very high quality on single-scene reconstruction.
