About Me

I am a final-year PhD candidate at UC San Diego, advised by Professor Ravi Ramamoorthi. In the past, I have had the fortune to work at the Max Planck Institute and the University of Oxford.

I spent 1.5 years at NVIDIA AI working with Koki Nagano. I'm currently at Google DeepMind as a Student Researcher with Pratul Srinivasan.

I'm lucky to be supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship and Jacobs School of Engineering Fellowship.

I'm currently looking for what's next!
